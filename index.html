Privacy Policy

Collection and Use of Information

We do not collect, store, use, or disclose any personal information to third parties. WeatherWave operates offline and does not require internet access for its functioning. All weather-related data is stored only locally on your device.

Location Access

WeatherWave may request access to your location for accurate determination of weather conditions in your region. However, we do not store information about your location and do not disclose it to third parties.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please periodically check this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
